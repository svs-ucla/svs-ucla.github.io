Our Mission

Society for Visiting Scholars at UCLA is a student-run club, established in 2019 by a group of passionate undergraduate students. We are here to share this passion with visiting scholars who come to UCLA to further their academic career and to help them to adjust to their life on campus more efficiently and meaningfully. We believe in the power of global connection in the field of academia and research; so we aim to build a platform for a freer communication between local scholars and scholars from around the world.
