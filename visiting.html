Regardless of the plan you select, all international scholars with a J1 visa must meet the insurance policy requirements below:

  • $100,000 USD per accident & illness in medical coverage
  • A deductible not to exceed $500 USD per accident or illness
  • Coinsurance that does not exceed 25%
  • Medical evacuation coverage must be at least $50,000 USD
  • Repatriation coverage must be at least $25,000 USD
  • Pre-existing conditions must be covered by the insurance policy with a waiting period no longer than 12 months.
  • Carrier must be at least A- rated or backed by the full faith and credit of the Exchange Visitor's government

Our Mission

Society for Visiting Scholars at UCLA is a student-run club, established in 2019 by a group of passionate undergraduate students. We are here to share this passion with visiting scholars who come to UCLA to further their academic career and to help them to adjust to their life on campus more efficiently and meaningfully. We believe in the power of global connection in the field of academia and research; so we aim to build a platform for a freer communication between local scholars and scholars from around the world.
